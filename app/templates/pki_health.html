What CertShield Collects for Health

CA certificate
Fetched with certutil -config <CAHost\CAName> -ca.cert.
CRL / CDP
Extracted from CA certificate CDP and CA\CRLPublicationURLs; HTTP CRLs are downloaded and parsed for nextUpdate.
AIA
Extracted from AIA extension and CA\CACertPublicationURLs; HTTP URLs are probed.
OCSP
OCSP URLs are extracted from AIA. If no URL exists, status is Not Configured, not Healthy.
Issued certificates
Queried per CA with certutil -view; the collector account needs CA database read access.
